## üßê Do You Need to Fix All Warnings?

Not always. While warnings don‚Äôt break your program, some are worth fixing.

### ‚úÖ Fix warnings when:
- They're about deprecated methods
- They hint at possible runtime errors (like null references)
- They reduce clarity or break best practices

### ‚ùå You can ignore warnings when:
- They're about unused vars in test or temporary code
- They're non-critical and don‚Äôt affect functionality
